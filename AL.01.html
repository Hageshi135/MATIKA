Nilai x yang memenuhi persamaan \[x^{2} + \frac{400}{x^{2} + 1}=39\]

Sumber: IG:@mantappuacademy

Cara Megerjakan

\[x^{2} + \frac{400}{x^{2} + 1}=39\]
kedua sisi kita tambah dengan 1\[1 + x^{2} + \frac{400}{x^{2} + 1}=39 +1\]
Misalkan M \[\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black} = x^{2} + 1 \] \[\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black} + \frac{400}{\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black}}=40\]
Kedua sisi kita kali dengan M \[(\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black} + \frac{400}{\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black}})\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black}=40(\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black})\] \[\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black}^{2}+400=40\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black}\]
Kedua sisi kita kurangi dengan 40M \[\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black}^{2}+400-40\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black} =40\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black}-40\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black}\] \[\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black}^{2}+400-40\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black} =0\]
Faktorkan \[(\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black}-20)^{2}=0\]
Satu-satunya cara agar pemangkatan sebuah faktor menjadi 0 adalah jika bilangan pokoknya adalah 0
(M - 20 = 0 ) \[\left(\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black} -20 \right)^{2}=0\longrightarrow \color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black}-20=0\] \[\color{#6d66d9}\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black}\color{Black}-20+20=0+20\] \[\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black}=2 0\]
Ingat \[\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black}=x^{2}+1\] \[\color{#6d66d9}M\color{Black}=20\longrightarrow x^{2}+1=20\] \[x^{2}+1-1=20-1\] \[x^{2}=19\] \[x =\pm 19\]
