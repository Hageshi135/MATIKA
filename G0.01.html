Rumus Refleksi

\[\text{Pencerminan terhadap sumbu x}:A\left( x,y \right)\to A'\left( x,-y \right)\] \[\text{Pencerminan terhadap sumbu y}:A\left( x,y \right)\to A'\left( -x,y \right)\] \[\text{Pencerminan terhadap garis y = x}:A\left( x,y \right)\to A'\left( y,x \right)\] \[\text{Pencerminan terhadap garis y = -x}:A\left( x,y \right)\to A'\left( -y,-x \right)\]
